TrackShare

Share your GPS tracks with anyone, anywhere

  • Upload GPX tracks from myTracks apps
  • View tracks on interactive maps
  • Share with anyone via a simple link
  • No registration required
  • Mobile-friendly interface

How to View a Track

To view a shared track, use the following URL format:
http://your-domain/track.html?id=TRACK_ID

Replace TRACK_ID with the unique identifier of your track.

TrackShare is part of the myTracks ecosystem
